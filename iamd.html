Big Data Engineering and Applications

"Yes, I've got some stocks from Oracle." Sabeur Aridhi

In this specialisation, you specialize yourself in the database. May it be understanding it, make it from nothing or being able to adapt it to an existing software, you will be able to do everything. Because all those work need a good understanding of all the softaware that rules database, it takes one year to get a grasp of it.

Being a member of this specialisation is far from easy, forget about sleeping and resting, you are here to work the hardest you can (and even more). You can learn more due to more project.

Inside the specialisation, you can choose between 3 ways : biomedical, smart cities, energy management.

What can I do after I graduate with such a specialisation ?

All the major actors of the market have database, they need us. Every company which possesses data and wants to exploit it might be interested in us.
