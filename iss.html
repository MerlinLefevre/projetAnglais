Internet Systems and Security

Internet is a wonderful platform which can connect billions of entities all over the world. Understanding it, its functionning and security is primordial to use it correctly and safely. That's what ISS students do.

What can I do after I graduate with such a specialisation ?

A lot of companies will open their doors if you are specialized in ISS. Here is a non-exhaustive list of fields you will have access to.

  • Banks, Insurance
  • Research and development
  • Inernet Services

What will I learn in ISS ?

Here is a list of the sujects you will learn in this specialisation

  • Information Theory
  • Data Compression and Trustworthiness (Compression des données et Codes Correcteurs d'erreurs)
  • Access Networks, Physical Networks
  • Performance Evaluation (Evaluation des performances)
  • Advanced networks and systems (Systèmes et Réseaux Avancés)
  • Cryptography and Authentication (Cryptograhie)
  • Digital Investigation and Forensics
  • Bootcamp

What do the students think of ISS ?

The ISS specialisation is often one the most wanted by students who neither want to take Software Engineering because of Compiler II, nor BDEA because there is too much maths. ES and EIS ? Never heard of that...

Some quotes of current ISS students :

I left BDEA for ISS, and almost got elected class representative in both of the specialisations. (Mathieu Trousseu)
Software engineering? I have thought of it, but come on, Compiler II... (Valentin Moreau)
ISS are really into JIG (Jeux Inter-Groupes or Inter Groups Games, ndt), last time we were, like, 1... (Valentin Moreau)
