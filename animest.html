Anim'Est

"Anim'Est in one word ? Friendliness" Most of the members

This association is about japanese culture. May it be through waifus, video games, or cosplay, they will do all they can to make you discover what they love. The main purpose of the association is a festival that will celebrate its 15 years of existence this year.

Being an active member of this associatioàn requires a lot of free time because there is a lot job to do. From contatcing VIPs to ensure the comfort of the visitors, many ressources are required.

Knows as a cult of Smash Bros, dicover the truth about this association.

Values

Friendliness : Always welcome each other, we are one in this association. Help each other, welcome every.

"Account should be taken of the need to achieve friendliness and high objectives in preparing the next edition." Raphaël Agathon

Smash Bros: Everyone should be a pro-player of smash bros. A break, lunch time, or free time, come play Smash Bros, it's not like we have projects to do.

"One more game before I go working on my projects." an unknown R2

Openness : welcoming everyone and every way of thinking no matter who is the person. New idea are welcome and more importantly we respect the work of everyone so that we always welcome new members.
