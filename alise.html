Alise

"Alise ? .......AH the thing with the graduates" Anyone in Telecom

This association ensure that the school and the students can keep contact with the graduates may it be for professsional matter or friendly matter. It also help the students with choosing a path they want for their future and prevent them from traps. They also make the school reputation grows and give a solid feed back about the after school life.

Another purpose of the association is to help students who are moving far away, they can have support, advices and all sorts of things you may need when moving. Every year, according to the school a large part of the graduate go abroad for large period of their professional career.

Finally, this association help the current students with finding an internship. It's more for second and third years but it provides a good support for everyone. It also supports the association by giving them human support or subventions.
