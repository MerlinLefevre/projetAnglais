Software Engineering

"Here, you create, install, configure, customize, integrate, support and maintain the software." François Charoy

In this specialisation, you will become a much better developer. Think, invent, or just try are common value for each member. Moreover, you can have a lot of free time to form yourself to whatever you like.

Being a member of the best speciasation is far from easy, other student might look down on us, it 's only envy. Here, you can do whatever you want as a developer which is the particularity of this specialisation. Moreover, we learn how to build from nothing, which is an excellent value for an engineer.

In this page, you will find all you need to know about Software Engineering at Telecom, may the good or the very good.

What can I do after I graduate with such a specialisation ?

Our first target are the Software publishers, the start-ups and and company from numeric service, but also all companies who want to build an ambitious development project. May it be a Bank or your own start-up, you can work there as long as you want it.

"BON" Dominique Mery
