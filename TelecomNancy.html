Telecom Nancy

Telecom Nancy is Ingineering school located in Viller-lès-Nancy in France. Today, the school is led by Oliver Festor who chose to enlighten the reputation of the school at his best. Here, you can find all you want to become a developer via its five specialisation : "IL" Software Engineering (the best of all) , "LE" Embedded Software, "SIE" Enterprise Information Systems, "IAMD" Big Data Engineering and Applications and "ISS" Internet Systems and Security.

The five values of the school

Expertise : become well-formed ingineer, work hard, play hard, and code hard. Every students must be as precise as possible in every domain he can.

"I think there is a great deal of expertise and experience available in Telecom which we can make use of." Paul Leroux

Innovation : thinking outside the box, each student must be able to do so. In their second year, the students may help some researcher on their work to better them-self and make use oh their inquisiveness.

"Telecom Nancy encourages innovation through the strengthening of intellectual property rights." Guillaume Koenig

Openness : welcoming everyone and every way of thinking no matter who is the person. New idea are welcome, we even learn to code in Scala.

"Increased transparency and openness has been a key trend in the Telecom in recent years, in terms of both access to documents and publicy of debates." Olivier Festor

Ethics :respect of the word and the law. this value is the key to have a software you can be proud of.

"If our technology is all about grace and peace, so too must be our ethics." Thomas Grassellini

Conviviality : everyone is welcomed at our school, even the students of other schools, so that we can benefit from the best of everyone.

"To use the words of Ivan Illich - conviviality must replace productivity: Ethical values should replace technical ones and we must learn to realise rather than merely materialise." Clémence Picquet

Learning at Telecom

We learn with experts in each domain that uderstand perfectely what it is to be a student. Anybody can enjoy his life here, with many activities and associative projects.

Sadly in our first and third year, we can only make a few projects, but fortunatly there is the second year ! You can have up to 10 project to better ourselves and learn more everyday. When teaching a rich content, the knowledge and experience of the learners should be taken into consideration, but one sould also be able to gain this experience.

In Only three years, each students can become an Ingineer and choose between starting his/her own company or join one already on the market.
